ABOUT ANANAS:

Ananas is a gaming equipment brand, founded in 2024 by a group of passionate programmers who wanted to rise with the brand
of their own. They had their own vision of wanting to make gaming a better experience to the gamers, and implemented the idea
in the little project of Ananas.

The brand specialized in keyboards, mice and headsets only as a startup. Since it's a new brand, it is experimenting with commonly
used equipment among gamers. As it got more recognition, the team is currently expanding and working on other different tech devices
to enhance the quality of any gaming experience, and to add a different flavor to the industry.

Enjoy your time exploring the brand more. Please look forward for the upcoming devices. Good gaming!
